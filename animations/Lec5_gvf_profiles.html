Subcritical flow (y₀ > y꜀) is controlled from downstream (disturbances travel upstream, Lec 1), so the profile is integrated upstream from the control. Supercritical flow is controlled from upstream and integrated downstream. Profiles approach the NDL asymptotically and meet the CDL vertically (GVF breaks down there — a jump or drop follows).
