Energy & critical depth

E₁ = y₁ + q²/(2gy₁²)
E₂ = y₂ + q²/(2gy₂²)
Critical depth y꜀ = (q²/g)^⅓
E_min = 1.5·y꜀
E = y + q²/(2gy²)  ·  y꜀ = (q²/g)^⅓
q = y₂·√( 2gy₁ / (1 + y₂/y₁) )  (E₁ = E₂, y₂ ≈ a)

Sluice gate rating (lab Eq. 10–11)

Q_ideal = b·a·√(2gy₁)
C_d = Q/Q_ideal
Frictionless gate: specific energy is conserved across the gate, so the two depths y₁ (subcritical) and y₂ (supercritical) are alternate depths — the same vertical line on the E–y diagram. y₂ ≈ a as in the lab manual.
