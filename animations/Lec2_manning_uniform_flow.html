Uniform flow: gravity along the bed exactly balances boundary friction, so depth and velocity are constant — bed, water surface and energy line are all parallel (S_f = S_w = S₀). yₙ is found by solving Manning's equation for depth; y꜀ from the critical-flow condition dE/dy = 0.
