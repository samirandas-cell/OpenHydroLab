Energy & critical depth

Over the hump the local specific energy is E(x) = E₁ − z(x): the operating point slides left along the fixed E–y curve. Once choked, the crest holds exactly critical flow — the hump has become the control. Downstream the flow continues supercritical (a jump would follow in a real channel — see the hydraulic-jump module).
